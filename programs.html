Our Programs

Summer Research Internship (SRI)

An immersive summer research experience that connects high school and undergraduate students with faculty mentors, professional development workshops, laboratory experiences, and opportunities to explore careers in science and research.

→

Florida Heart Research Scholars (FHRS)

A two-year research fellowship for SRI alumni entering FIU as first-year or transfer students. FHRS provides faculty mentorship, hands-on research experience, professional development, stipends, and funding support for undergraduate research.

→

Biomolecular Sciences Institute Undergraduate Research Fellowship (BSI)

A two-year fellowship supporting junior and senior undergraduate students conducting faculty-mentored research in biomedical science through research training, professional development, mentorship, and funding opportunities.
