Our Mission

The Office of Undergraduate Research and Internships (OURI) empowers students to engage in meaningful, mentored research, scholarship, and creative activities across all disciplines.

We foster a culture of inquiry, innovation, and academic excellence by providing resources, guidance, and opportunities that enrich undergraduate education and prepare students for impactful careers and lifelong learning.
