OURI Events

Showcasing undergraduate research, professional development, and scientific engagement across Florida International University.

Summer Research Poster Showcase

The Summer Research Poster Showcase is an annual event hosted by the Office of Undergraduate Research and Internships (OURI) that highlights the research achievements of undergraduate students and provides a platform for sharing summer research experiences with the FIU community. Open to Biology and Marine Biology students who have participated in a summer research program or internship, the showcase features a poster session highlighting student projects across a variety of scientific disciplines.

The inaugural showcase was held in 2025 and featured 15 student presenters who shared their research experiences and projects with faculty, peers, and members of the university community. Following the success of the first event, the Summer Research Poster Showcase will return in 2026, continuing to provide students with the opportunity to strengthen their scientific communication skills, showcase their scholarly achievements, and celebrate the impact of undergraduate research at FIU.

Annual OURI Biology Research Forum

The OURI Biology Research Forum is an annual research symposium held each December that celebrates undergraduate research and scholarly achievement within the biological sciences. The event is open to Biology and Marine Biology students, as well as students participating in research programs supported by OURI or engaged in faculty-mentored research experiences.

Students have the opportunity to present their work through both poster and oral presentations, allowing them to develop scientific communication skills and engage with peers, faculty, and the broader research community.

The forum continues to serve as a central platform for recognizing student contributions to research, highlighting undergraduate achievements, and fostering a strong culture of research and scholarly engagement within the FIU community.

STEMinism: Women in Science Seminar Series

The STEMinism: Women in Science Seminar Series celebrates and highlights the achievements, experiences, and contributions of women in STEM fields while inspiring the next generation of scientists and researchers. The annual seminar brings together women at various stages of their academic and professional careers to share their perspectives on navigating and succeeding in STEM.

The 14th Annual Women in Science Seminar featured a keynote address by Dr. Madelen Diaz, followed by a panel discussion with undergraduate students, graduate students, and practicing physicians who shared insights into their educational pathways, professional experiences, challenges, and accomplishments.

The seminar provided students with the opportunity to engage with inspiring role models, build professional connections, and gain valuable perspectives from women making an impact across STEM disciplines.
