Research Opportunities

Discover opportunities to engage in undergraduate research, receive funding, present your work, and connect with faculty mentors throughout your academic journey at FIU.

Semester Research Opportunities

Sprouting at FIU

A six-week, peer-led introductory research program offered each Fall and Spring semester in partnership with MIT. Students develop foundational research skills through literature review training while mentored by graduate students and experienced undergraduate researchers.

Benefits:

  • Six virtual one-hour sessions
  • Peer mentorship and networking
  • Research skill development
  • Literature review training
  • Presentation opportunity
  • Open to all majors

Research Awards & Funding

OURI Research Award

The OURI Research Award supports undergraduate students engaged in faculty-mentored research. Funding may be used for research supplies, equipment, software, travel, and other project-related expenses.

In the most recent award cycle, four undergraduate researchers received funding through support provided by the Florida Academy of Sciences. Award recipients used these funds to advance their research projects and scholarly activities.

Conference & Presentation Opportunities

OURI encourages students to share their research through campus, statewide, and national conferences.

  • Summer Research Poster Showcase (September)
  • OURI Biology Research Forum (December)
  • Florida Undergraduate Research Conference (FURC)
  • Undergraduate Research Conference at FIU (URFIU)
  • National Conference on Undergraduate Research (NCUR)
  • Annual Biomedical Research Conference for Minoritized Scientists (ABRCMS)

Students receive support with abstract preparation, poster design, presentation skills, and conference participation.

Faculty Laboratories & Research Assistant Positions

Research opportunities are available throughout the academic year in faculty laboratories across the Department of Biological Sciences and other disciplines at FIU.

Students are encouraged to explore faculty research interests, connect with potential mentors, and seek opportunities through volunteer positions, research assistantships, and independent research projects.
